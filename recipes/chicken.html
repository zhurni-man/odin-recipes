Fried Chicken

Description

Crispy, juicy, and spicy homemade fried chicken. Great with mashed potatoes and gravy.

Ingredients

  • 1 quart vegetable oil for frying
  • 4 1/3 cups of all purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons garlic salt
  • 1 tablespoon ground black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon poultry seasoning
  • 1 1/2 cups beer, or as needed
  • 2 egg yolks, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 (3 pound) whole chicken, cut into pieces

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a deep-fryer to 350 degrees F
  2. Mix together 3 cups flour, garlic salt, 1 tablespoon pepper, paprika, and poultry seasoning in a medium bowl
  3. Stir together 1 1/2 cups beer, remaining 1 1/3 cups flour, egg yolks, salt, and 1/4 teaspoon pepper in a separate bowl; thin mixture with more beer if needed
  4. Moisten chicken pieces with a little water, then dip in the dry mixture. Shake off excess and dip in wet mixture, then dip in the dry mixture once more.
  5. Fry chicken in hot oil until well-browned and internal temperature reaches 165 degrees F, 15 to 18 minutes.
  6. Remove chicken pieces and drain on paper towels before serving.
